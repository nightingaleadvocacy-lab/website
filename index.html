In the past decade alone, the world of health policy has undergone drastic changes. Millions of uninsured Americans lack adequate healthcare coverage, prices for basic medical procedures have skyrocketed, and the historic overturning of Roe V. Wade has eliminated the constitutional right to abortion for women.

As the future of the United States, our words and hunger for reform matter. We created Nightingale Advocacy: an organization that actively works to empower youth to take real and decisive political action. Ultimately, our passion and intellectualism bears responsibility for crafting a better tomorrow. Let’s make every step count.
